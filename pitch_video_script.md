# VyaparAI - 3-Minute Pitch Video Script (ET AI Hackathon 2026)

## 0:00 - 0:30 | The Problem
*(Screen: Presenter speaking, cutting to B-roll of stacks of paper invoices and stressed business owners)*
"India has over 90 million MSMEs. They are the backbone of our economy, yet they manage their finances like it's 2005. Late payments, fraudulent invoices, complex contracts, and cashflow instability cost Indian small businesses billions of rupees every year in avoidable financial losses. Affordable access to professional accounting or legal counsel remains out of reach for many. They are flying blind."

## 0:30 - 1:30 | The Solution (VyaparAI)
*(Screen: Seamless transition to VyaparAI's dark-mode, glassmorphism dashboard. Beautiful Recharts visualizations rendering in real-time)*
"Enter VyaparAI: Your GenAI Business Co-Pilot. We built an autonomous, multi-agent system addressing Problem Statement 3: Enterprise Cost Intelligence.
Instead of manual data entry, business owners simply drag and drop their invoices, bank statements, and contracts. 
*Screen recording shows document upload, instant processing.*
Behind the scenes, our intelligent Celery workers run OCR and GPT-4o structuring. Our **Spend Intelligence Agent** instantly flags a duplicate invoice and a 30% overcharge from a vendor. Meanwhile, our **Cashflow Agent** analyzes 90-day bank averages and unpaid invoices, preemptively alerting the user of an SLA-breaching cash crunch 14 days before it happens."

## 1:30 - 2:30 | The Magic (RAG Chat Demo)
*(Screen: Demo of the RAG Chat interface. Typing: "Which vendor is overcharging me compared to last quarter?")*
"But we didn't stop at dashboards. We built a RAG-powered Copilot. The intelligence layer embeds every document into a pgvector database. You can ask it anything.
*Voiceover reads out Copilot's response, showing the exact citations and alerts.*
The system doesn't just summarize; it initiates corrective actions, providing a quantifiable financial impact. It's a completely autonomous workflow health monitor and financial advisor."

## 2:30 - 3:00 | Impact & Scalability
*(Screen: The Impact Model math. "₹85,000 Crores Annually")*
"Our impact model shows an average MSME saving ₹80,000 per month by recovering cost leakages and avoiding late penalties. Scaled to just 1% of the market, that's thousands of crores saved. VyaparAI is an AI-first, multi-document intelligence platform built to serve millions. Thank you."
